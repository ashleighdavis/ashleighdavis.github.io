Music

I played the clarinet in band in high school from grade 8-12.
I don't have a favourite genre of music, but I tend to listen to a lot of 70's and 80's music.
I also tend to listen to the same songs over and over again until I get sick of them.

Gorillaz

Favourite Album: The Now Now
Favourite Song: Kansas
I've never seen them live, but they're at the top of the list.

Imagine Dragons

Favourite Album: Smoke + Mirrors
Favourite Song: Hopeless Opus
I've seen them in concert 3 times and they've been amazing each time.

The Killers

Favourite Album: Direct Hits
Favourite Song: The Man
I saw them instead of going to my work's Christmas party and it was definitely the better option.
